## Technical SEO Considerations

### **URL Structure Consistency:**
- All pages depth = 1 (flat structure)
- Include target keywords in URLs
- Connecticut geographic modifier in relevant URLs
- Consistent hyphen usage for readability

### **Internal Linking Strategy:**
- Contact Us linked from every page
- About Us linked from all service pages
- Location pages cross-linked to relevant service pages
- Resource hub pages linked to service pages for conversion

### **Schema Markup Priority:**
1. Local Business schema (all pages)
2. Service schema (service pages)
3. Organization schema (about page)
4. Review schema (testimonial page)

This prioritization ensures Symphony Core establishes market authority in Connecticut while building a conversion-optimized website that supports both immediate lead generation and long-term SEO growth.

# How to Search for Any Business in Connecticut

This guide explains how to find business records for any business registered in the state of Connecticut using the official state portal.

## Step 1: Access the Connecticut Business Search Portal

Navigate to the official Connecticut business search website:
**URL:** `service.ct.gov`

## Step 2: Search for the Business

1. Locate the search bar on the website
2. Enter the business name you want to search for
3. **Tip:** Use partial names or keywords for better results rather than the exact business name
   - The system performs "fuzzy search" which helps find businesses even with approximate names
   - Example: Instead of searching "Rotary Ski Shop LLC", try "Rotary Ski"

## Step 3: Review Search Results

The search results will display matching businesses with the following key information:
- **Registration Number**
- **Registered Address** 
- **Principal Name** (business owner/officer)
- **Agent Information**
- **Business Status** (Active/Inactive)

## Step 4: View Detailed Business Information

Click on any business result to access comprehensive details including:
- Complete business name
- Business ID/ALE number
- Formation date
- Business entity type (LLC, Corporation, etc.)
- Principal's residence address
- Managing member information

## Step 5: Access Business Documents

### Available Documents:
- **Annual Business Filings**
- **Change of Address Forms** 
- **Other official business documents**

### To Download Documents:
1. Scroll to the documents section
2. Click "View as PDF" next to the desired document
3. Download the PDF file to your device

## Additional Information Available

The business documents provide extra details such as:
- **NAICS Code** - Industry classification number for the business
- Historical business filings and changes
- Complete filing history

## Example Search

**Business:** Symphony Core
**Result:** The search returned detailed information including registration number, addresses, and available documents like annual filings and address change records.

## Tips for Successful Searches

- Use partial business names for broader results
- Try different keyword combinations if the exact name doesn't work
- Check the business status to confirm if it's currently active
- All documents are available as downloadable PDFs

## Important Notes

- This portal only contains businesses **registered in Connecticut**
- All information is official state records
- Documents are available in PDF format for download and printing

# Symphony Core Marketing Metrics Glossary

---

## Lead Generation & Capture

### Total Leads Captured

The complete count of new prospect contact records added to the CRM system within a specified time period, typically measured monthly or quarterly, representing all individuals who have provided contact information through any marketing channel.

**Categories**: Lead Generation, Volume Metrics, Performance Indicators  
**Abbreviations**: TLC  
**Variations**: Total Leads, Leads Captured, New Leads  
**Synonyms**: Lead Volume, Lead Count, New Contacts  
**Tags**: leads, capture, volume, crm, tracking

---

### Lead Capture Rate

The percentage of website visitors or landing page viewers who complete a form or provide contact information, calculated by dividing captured leads by total unique visitors and multiplying by 100.

**Categories**: Lead Generation, Conversion Metrics, Performance Indicators  
**Abbreviations**: LCR  
**Variations**: Capture Rate, Lead Conversion Rate  
**Synonyms**: Visitor-to-Lead Conversion, Form Completion Rate  
**Tags**: conversion, leads, capture, percentage, performance

---

### Cost Per Lead

The average marketing expenditure required to generate a single qualified lead, calculated by dividing total marketing spend by the number of leads captured during the same period.

**Categories**: Lead Generation, Financial Metrics, ROI Metrics  
**Abbreviations**: CPL  
**Variations**: Lead Cost, Average Lead Cost  
**Synonyms**: Lead Acquisition Cost, Marketing Cost Per Lead  
**Tags**: cost, leads, budget, roi, efficiency

---

### Leads By Source

The breakdown and categorization of captured leads according to their originating marketing channel, such as organic search, paid advertising, social media, referrals, or direct traffic.

**Categories**: Lead Generation, Attribution Metrics, Channel Performance  
**Abbreviations**: LBS  
**Variations**: Lead Source Distribution, Source Attribution  
**Synonyms**: Lead Attribution, Channel Leads, Traffic Source Leads  
**Tags**: source, attribution, channel, tracking, analysis

---

### Form Submission Rate

The percentage of landing page or website visitors who complete and submit a contact form, calculated by dividing total form submissions by total page views and multiplying by 100.

**Categories**: Lead Generation, Conversion Metrics, User Behavior  
**Abbreviations**: FSR  
**Variations**: Form Completion Rate, Submission Rate  
**Synonyms**: Form Conversion Rate, Form Fill Rate  
**Tags**: forms, conversion, submission, landing-page, behavior

---

### Landing Page Conversion Rate

The percentage of visitors to a specific landing page who complete the desired action, such as form submission or appointment booking, calculated by dividing conversions by total visitors and multiplying by 100.

**Categories**: Lead Generation, Conversion Metrics, Page Performance  
**Abbreviations**: LPCR, LCR  
**Variations**: Page Conversion Rate, LP Conversion  
**Synonyms**: Landing Page Performance, Page Conversion Percentage  
**Tags**: landing-page, conversion, performance, optimization, tracking

---

### Lead Qualification Rate

The percentage of captured leads that meet predefined criteria for sales readiness or quality standards, calculated by dividing qualified leads by total leads captured and multiplying by 100.

**Categories**: Lead Generation, Quality Metrics, Sales Readiness  
**Abbreviations**: LQR  
**Variations**: Qualification Rate, Lead Quality Rate  
**Synonyms**: Qualified Lead Percentage, Lead Scoring Rate  
**Tags**: qualification, quality, scoring, readiness, filtering

---

### Lead-to-Opportunity Conversion Rate

The percentage of captured leads that progress to become viable sales opportunities in the pipeline, calculated by dividing opportunities created by total leads and multiplying by 100.

**Categories**: Lead Generation, Pipeline Metrics, Sales Performance  
**Abbreviations**: LTOCR, L2O  
**Variations**: Lead Opportunity Rate, L2O Conversion  
**Synonyms**: Opportunity Conversion Rate, Lead-to-Opp Rate  
**Tags**: opportunity, conversion, pipeline, sales, progression

---

### Average Lead Score

The mean numerical value assigned to leads based on predefined scoring criteria such as engagement level, demographic fit, and behavioral indicators, used to prioritize follow-up activities.

**Categories**: Lead Generation, Quality Metrics, Automation  
**Abbreviations**: ALS  
**Variations**: Mean Lead Score, Lead Score Average  
**Synonyms**: Lead Quality Score, Average Score  
**Tags**: scoring, quality, prioritization, automation, metrics

---

### Time to Lead Qualification

The average duration measured in hours or days from initial lead capture to completion of qualification process and assignment of qualified or disqualified status.

**Categories**: Lead Generation, Efficiency Metrics, Process Performance  
**Abbreviations**: TTLQ, TLQ  
**Variations**: Qualification Time, Lead Processing Time  
**Synonyms**: Qualification Duration, Lead Review Time  
**Tags**: time, qualification, efficiency, speed, processing

---

## Engagement & Communication

### First Response Time

The average duration between initial lead capture or inbound inquiry and the first meaningful communication attempt or response from the business, typically measured in minutes or hours.

**Categories**: Engagement Metrics, Response Metrics, Customer Service  
**Abbreviations**: FRT  
**Variations**: Initial Response Time, Response Speed  
**Synonyms**: Time to First Response, Initial Contact Time  
**Tags**: response, speed, engagement, service, timing

---

### Contact Rate

The percentage of communication attempts that result in successful connection with the lead or prospect, calculated by dividing successful contacts by total contact attempts and multiplying by 100.

**Categories**: Engagement Metrics, Communication Efficiency, Outreach Performance  
**Abbreviations**: CR  
**Variations**: Connection Rate, Reach Rate  
**Synonyms**: Success Rate, Contact Success Percentage  
**Tags**: contact, connection, outreach, efficiency, success

---

### Email Open Rate

The percentage of delivered emails that are opened by recipients, calculated by dividing unique opens by total delivered emails and multiplying by 100, indicating subject line effectiveness and audience engagement.

**Categories**: Engagement Metrics, Email Marketing, Communication Performance  
**Abbreviations**: EOR  
**Variations**: Open Rate, Email Opens  
**Synonyms**: Message Open Rate, Email Engagement Rate  
**Tags**: email, open, engagement, marketing, deliverability

---

### Email Click-Through Rate

The percentage of email recipients who click on at least one link within the email content, calculated by dividing unique clicks by total delivered emails and multiplying by 100.

**Categories**: Engagement Metrics, Email Marketing, Action Metrics  
**Abbreviations**: CTR, ECTR  
**Variations**: Click Rate, Email CTR  
**Synonyms**: Link Click Rate, Email Click Rate  
**Tags**: email, clicks, engagement, ctr, action

---

### SMS Response Rate

The percentage of text message recipients who reply to or engage with SMS communications, calculated by dividing responses by total messages delivered and multiplying by 100, primarily used in US markets.

**Categories**: Engagement Metrics, SMS Marketing, Communication Performance  
**Abbreviations**: SRR  
**Variations**: Text Response Rate, SMS Engagement Rate  
**Synonyms**: Message Response Rate, Text Reply Rate  
**Tags**: sms, response, engagement, mobile, communication

---

### WhatsApp Response Rate

The percentage of WhatsApp message recipients who reply to or engage with communications sent via WhatsApp Business platform, primarily used in non-US markets where SMS limitations exist.

**Categories**: Engagement Metrics, Messaging Marketing, International Communication  
**Abbreviations**: WRR, WARR  
**Variations**: WhatsApp Engagement Rate, WA Response Rate  
**Synonyms**: Messaging Response Rate, WhatsApp Reply Rate  
**Tags**: whatsapp, response, messaging, international, engagement

---

### Conversation-to-Appointment Rate

The percentage of initiated conversations or communication threads that result in a scheduled appointment, calculated by dividing appointments booked by total conversations and multiplying by 100.

**Categories**: Engagement Metrics, Conversion Metrics, Booking Performance  
**Abbreviations**: CTAR, C2A  
**Variations**: Chat-to-Appointment Rate, Conversation Conversion  
**Synonyms**: Discussion-to-Booking Rate, Conversation Success Rate  
**Tags**: conversation, appointment, conversion, booking, success

---

### Average Touches Before Conversion

The mean number of contact attempts or interactions required before a lead completes a desired conversion action such as booking an appointment or making a purchase.

**Categories**: Engagement Metrics, Journey Metrics, Process Performance  
**Abbreviations**: ATBC  
**Variations**: Touches to Conversion, Average Contacts  
**Synonyms**: Contact Points to Conversion, Interaction Count  
**Tags**: touches, conversion, journey, engagement, frequency

---

### Follow-up Completion Rate

The percentage of scheduled or automated follow-up tasks that are successfully completed within the designated timeframe, calculated by dividing completed follow-ups by total scheduled follow-ups and multiplying by 100.

**Categories**: Engagement Metrics, Process Metrics, Task Management  
**Abbreviations**: FCR  
**Variations**: Follow-up Success Rate, Task Completion Rate  
**Synonyms**: Follow-up Execution Rate, Task Success Percentage  
**Tags**: follow-up, completion, tasks, automation, execution

---

### Nurture Sequence Engagement Rate

The percentage of contacts enrolled in automated nurture campaigns who actively engage with content through opens, clicks, or replies, indicating campaign effectiveness and audience interest.

**Categories**: Engagement Metrics, Automation Metrics, Campaign Performance  
**Abbreviations**: NSER  
**Variations**: Sequence Engagement Rate, Nurture Engagement  
**Synonyms**: Campaign Engagement Rate, Automation Response Rate  
**Tags**: nurture, sequence, engagement, automation, campaigns

## Appointment & Booking

**Abbreviations**: ABR  
**Variations**: Booking Rate, Appointment Conversion Rate  
**Synonyms**: Scheduling Rate, Appointment Success Rate

---

### Appointments Scheduled Per Month

The total count of new appointments added to the calendar within a monthly period, representing the volume of scheduled client interactions regardless of completion status.

**Categories**: Booking Metrics, Volume Metrics, Calendar Management  
**Abbreviations**: ASM, ASPM  
**Variations**: Monthly Appointments, Monthly Bookings  
**Synonyms**: Appointments Per Month, Monthly Schedule Count  
**Tags**: appointment, monthly, volume, calendar, scheduling

---

### Booking-to-Show Rate

The percentage of scheduled appointments where the prospect or client actually attends, calculated by dividing completed appointments by total scheduled appointments and multiplying by 100.

**Categories**: Booking Metrics, Attendance Metrics, Client Behavior  
**Abbreviations**: BTSR, BSR  
**Variations**: Show Rate, Attendance Rate  
**Synonyms**: Appointment Attendance Rate, Show-Up Rate  
**Tags**: booking, show, attendance, completion, behavior

---

### Cancellation Rate

The percentage of scheduled appointments that are cancelled by either party before the scheduled time, calculated by dividing cancellations by total appointments and multiplying by 100.

**Categories**: Booking Metrics, Retention Metrics, Client Behavior  
**Abbreviations**: CR  
**Variations**: Appointment Cancellation Rate, Cancel Rate  
**Synonyms**: Cancellation Percentage, Appointment Loss Rate  
**Tags**: cancellation, appointment, retention, behavior, loss

---

### Reschedule Rate

The percentage of scheduled appointments that are moved to a different date or time rather than being cancelled, calculated by dividing rescheduled appointments by total appointments and multiplying by 100.

**Categories**: Booking Metrics, Client Behavior, Calendar Management  
**Abbreviations**: RR  
**Variations**: Rescheduling Rate, Appointment Change Rate  
**Synonyms**: Postponement Rate, Date Change Rate  
**Tags**: reschedule, appointment, change, flexibility, calendar

---

### No-Show Rate

The percentage of scheduled appointments where the prospect or client fails to attend without prior cancellation, calculated by dividing no-shows by total scheduled appointments and multiplying by 100.

**Categories**: Booking Metrics, Attendance Metrics, Client Behavior  
**Abbreviations**: NSR  
**Variations**: No-Show Percentage, Absence Rate  
**Synonyms**: Non-Attendance Rate, Missed Appointment Rate  
**Tags**: no-show, attendance, appointment, behavior, loss

### Time From Lead to Appointment

**Abbreviations**: TFLA, L2A Time

### Calendar Utilization Rate

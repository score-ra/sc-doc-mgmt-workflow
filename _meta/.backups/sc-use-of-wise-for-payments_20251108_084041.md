# Wise International Payment Guide for Symphony Core Team Members

This guide explains how to set up Wise payments for team members, demonstrates why Wise provides the best value, and outlines the required information needed for seamless international transfers.

## Required Information for Wise Payments

All team members must provide the following details on their invoices to enable Wise transfers:

| Required Field            | Format/Example                                       |
|---------------------------|------------------------------------------------------|
| Account Holder Name       | Full legal name (exactly as on bank records)        |
| Account Number            | 12-16 digit bank account number                     |
| IFSC Code                 | 11-character bank branch code (e.g., PUNB0756800)   |
| Bank Name                 | Complete bank name (e.g., Punjab National Bank)     |
| Bank Address              | Full branch address including city and postal code  |
| Team Member Address       | Complete residential/business address with postal code |
| Email Address             | For payment confirmation and tracking updates       |

**Important:** Double-check all details for accuracy. Incorrect information may delay payments or result in failed transfers.

## Cost Comparison: Why Wise is Superior

| Payment Method | Transfer Fee          | Exchange Rate Markup | Total Cost      | Transfer Speed | INR Received |
|----------------|----------------------|---------------------|-----------------|----------------|--------------|
| **Wise**       | ~$1.90 (for $135)    | None (mid-market)   | 1.7-1.9% + $2.50 FIRC | Seconds | **Highest** |
| PayPal         | 4.4% + fixed fee     | 3-4% below market   | 7-9%+          | ~1 day         | Much lower   |
| Chase Wire     | $40-50 per transfer  | 2-3% FX margin      | 5-7%+          | 2-8 days       | Lower        |

### Real-World Example
For a $284 USD payment:
- **Wise**: Team member receives ~₹23,800 (after ~$5.50 total fees)
- **PayPal**: Team member receives ~₹21,500 (after ~$25+ total fees) 
- **Chase Wire**: Team member receives ~₹22,200 (after ~$45+ total fees)

**Wise delivers 10-15% more money to team members compared to alternatives.**

## Comparison with Other Methods

### Why Not PayPal?
- **High fees**: 4.4% + fixed fee + 3-4% exchange rate markup
- **Poor exchange rates**: 3-4% below market rate reduces INR received
- **Slower transfers**: Takes ~24 hours vs seconds with Wise
- **Total cost**: 7-9%+ of transfer amount vs 1.7-1.9% with Wise

### Why Not Traditional Wire Transfers?
- **Expensive fees**: $40-50 per transfer regardless of amount
- **Poor rates**: 2-3% exchange rate margin reduces payout
- **Slow processing**: 2-8 business days vs instant with Wise
- **Complexity**: Requires SWIFT codes and extensive documentation

Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades apply at next billing cycle.

You'll be billed for your selected plan. Cancel anytime during trial with no charges.

**What if I exceed my credits?**  
We alert you at 75% and 90% usage. Transparent overage rates apply automatically.

**Is the AI really unlimited?**  
Yes. Voice AI calls, conversations, reviews, and content are unlimited. Subject to fair use policy for extreme usage (>10,000 Voice AI minutes/month).

**Are there contracts?**  
Essential and Professional are month-to-month. Enterprise includes 12-month commitment.

## Churn Prevention

### Early Warning Signs
```
Red flags (check weekly):
- No logins 7+ days
- Zero automation runs
- No appointments booked
- Support tickets decreasing
- Usage declining month-over-month
```

### Intervention Protocol
```
Week 1: Friendly check-in email
Week 2: Personal call from AM
Week 3: Escalate, offer extra support
Before cancellation: Exit interview, offer downgrade
```

### Retention Offers (if necessary)
- 1 month free (saves $4K+ LTV)
- Plan downgrade
- Temporary suspension
- Additional training

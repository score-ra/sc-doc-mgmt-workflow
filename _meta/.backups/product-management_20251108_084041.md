# Product Management Glossary

The following glossary provides clear, concise definitions of the top 50 essential terms in product management. Each term includes relevant context to support understanding and effective communication within product management operations.

---

### A/B Testing  
A method of comparing two versions of a webpage or app to determine which performs better.  
Categories: Testing, Analytics  
Abbreviations: NA  
Variations: AB Testing  
Synonyms: Split Testing  
Tags: testing, optimization, conversion, analytics, experiment

### Acceptance Criteria  
The conditions that a software product must satisfy to be accepted by a user, customer, or stakeholder.  
Categories: Quality, Development  
Abbreviations: NA  
Variations: NA  
Synonyms: Requirements, Conditions of Acceptance  
Tags: requirements, development, quality, validation, specs

### Agile  
A project management and product development approach that prioritizes flexibility and collaboration.  
Categories: Methodology, Processes  
Abbreviations: NA  
Variations: NA  
Synonyms: Agile Methodology  
Tags: project-management, methodology, collaboration, scrum, development

### API (Application Programming Interface)  
A set of rules and protocols for building and interacting with software applications.  
Categories: Technology, Integration  
Abbreviations: API  
Variations: NA  
Synonyms: Interface  
Tags: integration, software, development, technology, data

### Artifact  
A document or tool used in Agile frameworks like Scrum, such as product backlog or sprint backlog.  
Categories: Agile, Documentation  
Abbreviations: NA  
Variations: NA  
Synonyms: Work Item, Output  
Tags: agile, scrum, documentation, process, tool

### Backlog  
An ordered list of work to be done, typically features, bug fixes, or technical tasks.  
Categories: Planning, Agile  
Abbreviations: NA  
Variations: Product Backlog, Sprint Backlog  
Synonyms: Task List  
Tags: agile, planning, tasks, prioritization, backlog

### Beta Testing  
A phase of testing where a product is released to a limited audience outside the company prior to general release.  
Categories: Testing, Quality  
Abbreviations: NA  
Variations: NA  
Synonyms: User Acceptance Testing (UAT)  
Tags: testing, quality, release, feedback, validation

### Buyer Persona  
A semi-fictional representation of an ideal customer based on market research and real data about existing customers.  
Categories: Marketing, Customer Insights  
Abbreviations: NA  
Variations: Customer Persona  
Synonyms: User Persona  
Tags: customer, persona, marketing, segmentation, target-audience

### Churn Rate  
The percentage of customers who stop using a product during a specific period.  
Categories: Metrics, Customer  
Abbreviations: NA  
Variations: Customer Churn  
Synonyms: Attrition Rate  
Tags: metrics, customer, retention, analytics, KPI

### Customer Journey Map  
A visual representation of the customer’s experience with a product or service across all touchpoints.  
Categories: Customer Experience, Design  
Abbreviations: CJM  
Variations: NA  
Synonyms: Journey Mapping  
Tags: customer, journey, mapping, UX, experience

### Customer Segmentation  
Dividing the market into distinct groups of customers based on demographics, behavior, or needs.  
Categories: Marketing, Customer  
Abbreviations: NA  
Variations: Market Segmentation  
Synonyms: User Segmentation  
Tags: marketing, segmentation, customer, analytics, targeting

### Definition of Done (DoD)  
A clear and concise statement of the criteria a product increment must satisfy to be accepted by the team.  
Categories: Agile, Quality  
Abbreviations: DoD  
Variations: NA  
Synonyms: Done Criteria  
Tags: agile, quality, criteria, sprint, completion

### Feature  
A distinct element or function of a product that provides value to users.  
Categories: Product, Development  
Abbreviations: NA  
Variations: NA  
Synonyms: Functionality  
Tags: product, feature, development, value, functionality

### Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy  
A plan that outlines how a company will launch a product to the market and achieve competitive advantage.  
Categories: Strategy, Marketing  
Abbreviations: GTM  
Variations: NA  
Synonyms: Launch Strategy  
Tags: strategy, marketing, launch, planning, sales

### Hypothesis  
A proposed explanation or assumption that guides product experiments or decisions.  
Categories: Research, Testing  
Abbreviations: NA  
Variations: NA  
Synonyms: Assumption  
Tags: research, hypothesis, testing, experiment, validation

### Increment  
The sum of all product backlog items completed during a sprint plus the increments of all previous sprints.  
Categories: Agile, Development  
Abbreviations: NA  
Variations: NA  
Synonyms: Product Increment  
Tags: agile, development, sprint, delivery, increment

### Iteration  
A timeboxed period in which product development teams deliver a working product increment.  
Categories: Agile, Development  
Abbreviations: NA  
Variations: Sprint  
Synonyms: Development Cycle  
Tags: agile, iteration, sprint, delivery, development

### Kano Model  
A theory for product development categorizing customer preferences into basic needs, performance, and excitement factors.  
Categories: Customer Insights, Strategy  
Abbreviations: NA  
Variations: NA  
Synonyms: NA  
Tags: strategy, customer, satisfaction, model, prioritization

### Key Performance Indicator (KPI)  
A measurable value that indicates how effectively a company is achieving key business objectives.  
Categories: Metrics, Business  
Abbreviations: KPI  
Variations: NA  
Synonyms: Performance Metric  
Tags: metrics, business, performance, analytics, goals

### Key Result (KR)  
A measurable outcome used to track progress toward an objective in the OKR framework.  
Categories: Strategy, Metrics  
Abbreviations: KR  
Variations: NA  
Synonyms: Outcome  
Tags: OKR, strategy, metrics, performance, goals

### Landing Page  
The first page a visitor lands on after clicking a link, designed to prompt a specific action.  
Categories: Marketing, UX  
Abbreviations: NA  
Variations: NA  
Synonyms: Destination Page  
Tags: marketing, landing, conversion, UX, web

### Lifecycle  
The series of stages a product goes through from inception to end-of-life.  
Categories: Product, Strategy  
Abbreviations: NA  
Variations: Product Lifecycle  
Synonyms: Life Cycle  
Tags: product, lifecycle, stages, development, strategy

### Localization  
Adapting a product to meet the language, cultural, and other specific needs of a particular region.  
Categories: Product, Market  
Abbreviations: NA  
Variations: NA  
Synonyms: Internationalization (with a broader scope)  
Tags: product, localization, market, adaptation, culture

### Market Fit  
The degree to which a product satisfies a strong market demand.  
Categories: Strategy, Product  
Abbreviations: NA  
Variations: Product-Market Fit  
Synonyms: PMF  
Tags: market, fit, product, demand, validation

### Market Research  
Gathering and analyzing information about consumers' needs, preferences, and market conditions.  
Categories: Research, Marketing  
Abbreviations: NA  
Variations: NA  
Synonyms: Market Analysis  
Tags: research, marketing, consumer, data, analysis

### Milestone  
A significant event or achievement in the product development or project timeline.  
Categories: Planning, Project Management  
Abbreviations: NA  
Variations: NA  
Synonyms: Key Event  
Tags: milestone, planning, project, timeline, achievement

### Minimum Viable Product (MVP)  
A version of a product with just enough features to validate assumptions and gather user feedback.  
Categories: Product, Development  
Abbreviations: MVP  
Variations: NA  
Synonyms: NA  
Tags: product, MVP, development, validation, feedback

Categories: Design, Development  
Abbreviations: NA  
Variations: NA  
Synonyms: Prototype (low fidelity)  
Tags: design, mockup, prototype, UX, visualization

Abbreviations: NA  
Variations: NA  
Synonyms: NA

Categories: Metrics, Customer  
Abbreviations: NPS  
Variations: NA

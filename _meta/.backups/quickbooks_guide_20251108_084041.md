## 2. Standardized Tags & Categories

### Customer Tags (For Income Tracking)
- **New-Client** - First 90 days of relationship
- **Recurring-Monthly** - Monthly retainer clients
- **Project-Based** - One-time project work
- **Setup-Fee** - Implementation services
- **CT-Local** - Connecticut-based clients
- **National** - Out-of-state clients

### Expense Categories
- **Core-Platform** - Essential SaaS tools (GoHighLevel, QuickBooks, etc.)
- **AI-Automation** - AI tools and automation platforms
- **Client-Delivery** - Direct client service costs
- **Business-Development** - Growth and expansion costs
- **Administrative** - General business operations

### Project/Location Tags
- **CT-Market** - Connecticut market activities
- **National-Expansion** - National market development
- **Product-Development** - Platform enhancements
- **Internal-Operations** - Company infrastructure

## 3. Monthly Financial Review Process

### Week 1 of Each Month
1. **Reconcile all accounts** - Bank, credit cards, PayPal
2. **Review GoHighLevel integration** - Ensure all invoices synced
3. **Categorize transactions** - Apply consistent tags/categories
4. **Update recurring transactions** - Verify subscription amounts

### Week 2 of Each Month
1. **Generate standard reports**:
   - Profit & Loss by Category
   - Balance Sheet
   - Cash Flow Statement
   - Sales by Customer Summary
2. **Review expense categories** - Ensure alignment with budget
3. **Update business plan metrics** - Revenue, expenses, growth rates

## 4. Business Plan Integration

### Key Metrics to Track
- **Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)** - Track via customer tags
- **Customer Acquisition Cost** - Marketing expenses / new customers
- **Average Contract Value** - Total revenue / number of clients
- **Technology Cost Ratio** - Platform costs as % of revenue
- **Contractor Utilization** - Professional services as % of revenue

### Annual Business Plan Reporting
QuickBooks should provide these data points for business plan updates:
- Historical revenue by service type
- Operating expense trends by category
- Technology infrastructure costs
- Contractor and professional services spending
- Cash flow patterns and seasonality

## 5. Compliance & Best Practices

### Tax Preparation Support
- **Quarterly Reviews** - Ensure all deductions properly categorized
- **1099 Tracking** - Monitor contractor payments (>$600 annually)
- **Asset Depreciation** - Track equipment and software purchases
- **Expense Documentation** - Maintain receipts for all business expenses

### Data Security
- **Two-Factor Authentication** - Enable on QuickBooks account
- **Regular Backups** - QuickBooks Online auto-backup sufficient
- **Access Control** - Limit user permissions as team grows
- **Integration Monitoring** - Verify GoHighLevel sync monthly

## 6. Simple Start Plan Optimization

### Current Limitations & Workarounds
- **Limited Users**: One user maximum - use accountant access for CPA
- **No Time Tracking**: Use ClickUp for project time, summarize in QB
- **No Inventory**: Not needed for service business model
- **Basic Reporting**: Supplemented by GoHighLevel CRM analytics

### When to Consider Upgrading
- **Multiple Users Needed** - When hiring full-time employees
- **Advanced Reporting** - If business plan requires detailed analytics
- **Project Profitability** - When tracking per-client profitability becomes critical
- **Integration Expansion** - If additional platform integrations required

# Symphony Core Prospecting Video Script

**Short video script for social media, ads, and prospecting campaigns**

---

## Metadata

- **Duration:** 60-90 seconds
- **Target Audience:** Cold prospects, social media viewers
- **Platform:** LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram, YouTube ads
- **Call to Action:** Schedule discovery call or visit website
- **Recording Format:** Screen recording OR talking head + B-roll
- **Resolution:** 1080p square (1:1) for social, or 16:9 for YouTube

---

## Production Notes

### Recording Setup
- **Format Options:**
  - Option A: Talking head (presenter on camera)
  - Option B: Screen recording with voiceover
  - Option C: Hybrid (presenter + screen)
- **Duration:** Keep under 90 seconds for social media
- **Hook:** First 3 seconds critical (grab attention)
- **Captions:** Essential (most watch without sound)

### Platform Variations
- **LinkedIn:** Professional tone, 1:1 or 16:9
- **Facebook/Instagram:** Square 1:1, casual tone
- **YouTube Ads:** 16:9, skippable after 5 seconds
- **Email:** Thumbnail + embedded video

## Script Version 1: Problem-Solution (60 seconds)

### Opening Hook (0-3 seconds)
**Visual:** Text overlay "Are you losing leads?"
**Voiceover:**
```
Stop me if this sounds familiar...
```

**Production Notes:**
- Pattern interrupt - grab attention immediately
- Text on screen for silent viewers
- Presenter looks directly at camera

---

### The Problem (3-20 seconds)
**Visual:** Show stressed business owner OR chaotic inbox/calendar
**Voiceover:**
```
A lead fills out your website form at 9 PM. You see it the next morning. You call them at 11 AM. They don't answer. You try again tomorrow. No response. They already hired your competitor.

This happens every single day, and it's costing you thousands of dollars in lost revenue.
```

**Production Notes:**
- Show visual of ignored leads
- Frustrated/empathetic tone
- Captions showing key points

---

### The Solution (20-45 seconds)
**Visual:** Symphony Core logo + clean automation interface
**Voiceover:**
```
What if you could respond to every lead instantly, 24/7, even while you sleep?

That's what Symphony Core does. We automate your lead follow-up, appointment scheduling, and customer communication - so you never miss an opportunity again.

No more lost leads. No more manual follow-up. Just automatic systems working for your business around the clock.
```

**Production Notes:**
- Shift to upbeat, confident tone
- Show clean, simple automation visual
- Highlight "instant" and "automatic"

---

### The Call to Action (45-60 seconds)
**Visual:** Contact info, calendar link, website URL
**Voiceover:**
```
Ready to automate your small business? Click the link below to schedule a free 15-minute discovery call. We'll show you exactly how Symphony Core can save you time and grow your revenue.

No obligation. No pressure. Just results.
```

**Production Notes:**
- Display clickable link prominently
- Show calendar icon or booking button
- Confident, inviting tone
- End with logo and contact info

## Best Practices for Social Media Video

### Hook (First 3 Seconds)
- Start with question or shocking statement
- Use pattern interrupt (stop scrolling)
- Display text overlay (silent viewing)
- Show faces (increases engagement)

### Middle (Keep Attention)
- Fast pacing (cut every 3-5 seconds)
- Visual variety (don't show same shot too long)
- Use captions (80% watch without sound)
- Highlight key numbers/stats

### End (Call to Action)
- Clear, specific next step
- Display link or contact info
- Use verbal + visual CTA
- Create urgency (limited time, limited spots)

### Technical
- Square 1:1 for feed posts
- Vertical 9:16 for Stories/Reels
- Horizontal 16:9 for YouTube/ads
- Keep under 60 seconds for organic social
- First frame = thumbnail (make it count)

## Metrics to Track

### Engagement Metrics
- View rate (views / impressions)
- Watch time (average % watched)
- Completion rate (% who watched to end)
- Click-through rate (clicks / views)

### Conversion Metrics
- Discovery calls booked
- Website visits from video
- Form submissions
- Cost per lead (for ads)

### Social Metrics
- Likes, comments, shares
- Follower growth
- Tag/mentions
- Saves/bookmarks
